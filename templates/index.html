About Us

Borgagården has been an inn since 1948 when Linnea Näslund built the first part of the main building, which today is used for our fine dining restaurant & saloon pub

Fine-Dining

The restaurant, with a captivating panorama view out over Borgahällan, Borgasjön & the mountains. Here you eat well made food, made from locally hand picked ingredients.
Must be booked before 8PM the day before

Saloon Pub

The saloon, with a wild west theme, Borgafjäll's mountain chain right outside the sheriffs door. Here you have a good variaty of big and local beers, sit down & relax in front of our open fireplace.
Drop-in from 5PM everyday
